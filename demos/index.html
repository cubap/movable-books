Demos

Lightweight interactive experiments to illustrate movable book behaviors—no full IIIF viewer required.

  • Fold crossfade slider

    Blend between closed and open photographs of a flap to visualize the transformation.

  • Fold flip (manifest-driven)

    Animate a circular flap flipping using hinge and region data parsed from the local IIIF manifest.

  • Recto–Verso flip (Imperiia 315)

    Flip a card between front and back using a derivative manifest that crops the source image into two sides.
